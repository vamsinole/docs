### Step 3: Use filters to find call logs

Filter your call logs using these options:

- **From:** Enter the number from which you received the call.
- **To:** Enter the number to which the call was initiated.
- **Session ID:** Retrieve logs by entering the session ID.
- **AI Agent:** Filter logs by selecting a specific agent.
- **Date:** Filter by date range (From and To).
- **Status:** Filter by call status:
  - **ALL**
  - **Started:** Call has just started.
  - **Completed by user:** Call ended by the user.
  - **Busy:** AI tried to call but the user was busy.
  - **Ringing:** Call is initiated and ringing.
  - **Completed by agent:** Call ended by the agent.
  - **No answer:** AI initiated the call but the user did not pick up.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/filter.jpg" alt="Filter Calls" />

You see multiple call entries in the inbox. Filter, listen to calls, and sort by newest or oldest entries as needed.
