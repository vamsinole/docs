## Step-by-step guide

### 1. Set up your Google Sheets trigger
- **Trigger:** New Row
- **Connection:** `google-sheets`
- **Spreadsheet:** Select the sheet where you collect content ideas or submissions.
- **Include Team Drive Sheets:** Choose whether to include Team Drives.
- **Sheet:** Pick the specific sheet to monitor.

### 2. Configure the Contactswing SMS action ðŸ’¬
- In Contactswing, select **SMS** as the response type.
- Enter your Workspace API KEY.
- Add the Webhook URL.
- When a new row is added or modified, Contactswing triggers this action automatically.

### 3. Activate your Agentic AI âš¡
- Activate the flow after you configure both the Google Sheets trigger and Contactswing action.
- Every new Google Sheet submission now automatically triggers an SMS to the user.

---

## Example scenario
- A customer fills out a Google Form to request a callback.
- The form submission triggers the Agentic AI.
- Contactswing sends an SMS confirming the request to the customer.

## Benefits
- **Automation:** You don't need manual intervention.
- **Timely responses:** Customers receive instant acknowledgment.
- **Customizable:** Tailor SMS messages based on form responses.
