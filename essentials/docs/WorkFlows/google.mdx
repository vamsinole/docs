## Step-by-step guide

### 1. Connect Google Forms to your Agentic AI
- Go to Google Forms and select the form you want to use as a trigger.
- The trigger activates when a new response is submitted.
- Specify which form fields (e.g., Name, Email, Phone Number) will be used in the action.

### 2. Configure the Contactswing action
- In Contactswing, choose **SMS Action** as the response type.
- Set up your SMS template using dynamic data from the form (e.g., customer name, submission details).
- Example SMS: "Hello [Customer Name], your request has been received. We will get in touch with you shortly. Thank you for your submission!"
- Configure SMS settings:
  - **Sender Name**: Customize the sender name.
  - **SMS Frequency**: Choose instant or delayed sending.

### 3. Activate your Agentic AI
- Activate the flow after configuring both the Google Form trigger and Contactswing action.
- Now, every new Google Form submission will automatically trigger an SMS to the user.

## Example scenario

- Contactswing sends an SMS confirming the request to the customer.
