# Airparser to Contactswing Call Agentic AI

Automate your customer engagement by connecting **Airparser** to **Contactswing**. This guide shows you how to trigger a **phone call** in Contactswing automatically whenever a document is parsed in Airparser, so you can deliver instant, personalized responses.

## Step-by-step guide

### 1. Set up your Airparser webhook trigger üìù

- In Airparser, go to **Integrations** and click the **Webhook Connect** button.
- Select **Webhook Trigger** and paste the Webhook URL provided by Contactswing.
- Click **Create Webhook** to finalize the connection.
- Every time a document is parsed in Airparser, this trigger will activate the flow.

---

### 2. Configure the Contactswing Call action üìû

- In Contactswing, select **CALL** as the response type.
- Enter your **Workspace API Key**.
- Add the **Webhook URL** provided by Contactswing.
- The system will automatically initiate a phone call whenever a document is parsed in Airparser.

---

### 3. Activate your Agentic AI Flow ‚ö°

- Review all settings.
- Click **Publish** to activate the flow.
- Once published, the system will trigger the call automatically after every document parsing.

---

## Example scenario

- A document is parsed in Airparser containing customer details.
- This event triggers the Agentic AI flow.
- Contactswing automatically initiates a call to the specified recipient.
