## Step-by-step guide

Create an AI-powered email agent to automate your email processes and improve customer interactions.

### Step 1: Access the AI Agent dashboard

Navigate to the **AI Agent** tab and click **"New AI Agent"** to start creating your email automation agent.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/Create-Assistant.jpg" alt="AI Agent" />
</Frame>






### Step 2: Choose Email agent type

Select **Email** from the agent type options to create an AI agent specifically designed for email communication management.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/emailtype.jpg" alt="AI Agent" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Configure your agent settings

Name your agent and configure these essential settings:

**Name**  
Enter a descriptive name for your email agent (e.g., "Customer Support Email Agent").

**GenAI Response**  
Choose your preferred AI response type. Select **Semi-Touch** for human-like interactions that enhance customer service quality.

**Knowledge Base**  
Add or select a knowledge base to provide your agent with up-to-date information for generating informed responses.

**Email ID**  
Enter the email address your agent will use (e.g., support@yourcompany.com).

**Email Setup**  
Configure responder settings and specify folders or labels for organizing emails efficiently.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/EAs2.jpg" alt="AI Agent" />
</Frame>

### Step 4: Complete configuration

Review your settings and click **Next** to proceed to the final setup step.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/EAs3.jpg" alt="AI Agent" />
</Frame>



### Step 5: Define your agent's prompt

Create the initial prompt that guides your agent's responses. Use the test option to verify your prompt works correctly.

**Writing effective prompts:**

- **Be specific** - Clearly state what you want your agent to accomplish
- **Use natural language** - Write conversationally to maintain personalized communication
- **Include context** - Provide relevant background information for accurate responses

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/EAs4.jpg" alt="AI Agent" />
</Frame>

**Example prompt template:**
```
You are a professional customer support email agent. Respond to customer inquiries in a friendly, helpful manner. Always acknowledge their concern, provide clear solutions, and offer additional assistance when appropriate. Keep responses concise but thorough.
```

## Next steps

Your Email AI Agent is ready! Go to the **Emails** section to start generating automated responses and streamline your email communication.
