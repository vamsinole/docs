## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to create an SMS campaign:

### 1. Define campaign details

Click **Create Campaign** to open the setup form and enter the required details:
- **Campaign Name**: (e.g., "Holiday Offer SMS Campaign")
- **Business Goal**: (e.g., "Increase foot traffic to store")

Example interface:

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign" />

### 2. Configure touch points

Set up the touchpoints for your campaign:
- **Touch Point Name**: Name the SMS step.
- **Type**: Select "SMS" as the mode of communication.
- **AI Agent**: Choose an AI-powered assistant if applicable.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign Touchpoint" />

### 3. Set scheduling & execution

Configure the execution settings:
- **Schedule Date**: Choose when the SMS will be sent.
- **Execution Days**: Select the active days for sending SMS.
- **Time Window**: Define the time window for SMS sends (e.g., 9 AM - 6 PM).
- **Delay Between SMS**: Set time gaps between individual SMS sends to avoid overloading.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign Scheduling" />

### 4. Select customers

Choose the customers for your campaign:
- **Select Data**: Choose from your list of available phone numbers and customer data.
- **Select Customers**: Filter or choose the customers you want to target.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc1.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign Customers" />

### 5. Configure SMS content

Set up the content for your SMS campaign:
- **SMS Content**: Write the message that will be sent to the customers.
- **Character Limit**: Ensure the SMS content stays within the 160 character limit.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc2.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign Content" />

### 6. Finalize & launch campaign

Review your settings and finalize the campaign:
- **Preview SMS**: Review a sample SMS to ensure accuracy.
- **Submit Campaign**: Click **Create** to launch your SMS campaign.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sc2.jpg" alt="SMS Campaign Launch" />

## SMS campaigns interface

The interface displays all created SMS campaigns with their details, including options to view status and delete campaigns as needed.
