## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to create your RTB action:

### 1. Select RTB action

Choose **Real-Time Booking Action** from the available action types to get started.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/at.jpg" alt="RTB Action" />

### 2. Configure your RTB action

Fill in the required fields:
- **Name**: Enter a name for your action (e.g., "My Real-Time Booking Action").
- **Description**: Briefly describe your RTB action (e.g., "Action for scheduling bookings during calls").
- **Schedule a Booking**: Specify the service or person to book with (e.g., "Google Calendar").

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/google1.jpg" alt="Google Calendar Example" />

### 3. Create the action

After completing all fields and connecting your Google Calendar, click **Create Action** to save your real-time booking action. üéâ
