## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to create your Shopify action:

### 1. Select Shopify action

Choose **Shopify Action** from the available action types to get started.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sh1.jpg" alt="Shopify Action" />

### 2. Configure your Shopify action

Fill in the required fields:
- **Name**: Enter a name for your action (e.g., "Fetch Shopify Product & Order Details").
- **Description**: Briefly describe your Shopify action (e.g., "Action for fetching product and order information during calls").
- **Select Action Type**: Choose the action type to fetch product details, order status, or order timeline (e.g., "Fetch Product Details", "Fetch Order Status", "Fetch Order Timeline").
- **Ensure Shopify Integration is Active**: Make sure your Shopify account is connected through integrations before proceeding.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sh2.jpg" alt="Shopify Integration" />

### 3. Create the action

After configuring your Shopify action, click **Create Action** to save your settings. üéâ
