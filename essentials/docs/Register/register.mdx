## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to sign up for ContactSwing:

### 1. Start your free trial

Click **Start Free Trial** on the ContactSwing registration page.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s1.jpg" alt="Start Free Trial" />

### 2. Enter your email and password

Fill in your email address and a password of your choice.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s2.jpg" alt="Enter Email and Password" />

### 3. Verify your email

After submitting your details, check your email for an OTP (One-Time Password). Enter the OTP to verify your email address.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s3.jpg" alt="Verify Email" />

### 4. Sign in

Once your email is verified, you are redirected to the sign-in page. Enter your email and password to log in.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s4.jpg" alt="Sign In" />

### 5. Complete your registration

Click **Register** after entering your details. You will be taken to the subscription page.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s5.jpg" alt="Complete Registration" />

### 6. Choose a plan

Select a subscription plan that fits your needs. Special offers and discounts may be available.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/s6.jpg" alt="Choose a Plan" />

### 7. Finalize registration

Proceed to payment to complete your registration and start using ContactSwing AI Voice Assistant.
