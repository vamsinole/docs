Upload and manage files in your Knowledge Base to provide your AI agents with relevant documents. This guide shows you how to add files step by step.

## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add files to your Knowledge Base:

### 1. Add a new file

Click **New File** to start uploading documents related to your knowledge base.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/kbtype.jpg" alt="Add file" />

### 2. Upload your files

Drag your file into the designated area, then click **Add Files** to upload.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/upload.jpg" alt="Upload file" />

### 3. Confirm file addition

After dragging your file, make sure you click **Add Files** to complete the process.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/af.jpg" alt="Confirm file addition" />

### 4. View your files

Once your files are added, view them by clicking on the file name.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/af.jpg" alt="View added files" />
