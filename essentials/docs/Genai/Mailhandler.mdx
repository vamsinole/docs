## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to configure your incoming emails:

### 1. Connect your Gmail account

Go to the **Integrations** section and select the Google integration. Connect your Gmail account to enable email classification and automated responses.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/integra.jpg" alt="Google integration" />

### 2. Access the Email Assistant

After connecting, an Email Assistant is created automatically. Go to the Emails section, open the dropdown, and select the Email Assistant.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/incom2.jpg" alt="Access Email Assistant" />

### 3. Enable and configure your assistant

- **User's Full Name:** Enter your full name.
- **Select Type:** Choose "Draft" or "Send" to specify how responses are handled.
- **Instructions:** Provide clear instructions for your AI agent.
- **Knowledge Base:** Select a knowledge base for the agent to retrieve information.
- **Assistant Status:** Enable the assistant to start classifying and responding to emails. You can deactivate it anytime.
- **Advanced Settings:**
  - **Label Configuration:** Set up custom labels and options to organize emails.
  - **Email Rules:** Define rules with instructions and target email addresses. The AI will respond automatically to emails from those addresses.
