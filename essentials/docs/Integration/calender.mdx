# Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the Calendar integration:

### Step 1: Go to the Integrations section
Go to the **Integrations** section in your application. You'll find a list of external services. Select **Calendar** to start the setup process.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/04.jpg" alt="Integrations" />

### Step 2: Sign in with your Google Account
Click **Sign in with Google** to log in with your Google Account credentials. This redirects you to the Google login page, where you securely enter your details.
- Make sure to grant the necessary permissions for the integration to access your calendar data.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/05.jpg" alt="Google Sign-in" />

### Step 3: Authorize the integration
After logging in, you're prompted to authorize the connection. Allow the platform to access your calendar for seamless scheduling capabilities.
