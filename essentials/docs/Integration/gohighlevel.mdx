# Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the GoHighLevel integration:

### Step 1: Go to the Integrations section
Go to the **Integrations** section in your application. You'll find a variety of external services that can be connected. Select **GoHighLevel** to start the integration process.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/11.jpg" alt="Integrations" />

### Step 2: Sign in to your GoHighLevel account
If you're not already signed in, you'll see a prompt to **Sign in to GoHighLevel**. Click the button to log in with your GoHighLevel credentials. This redirects you to the GoHighLevel login page.
- Ensure you have the necessary permissions to allow integration access.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/gohighlevel-login.jpg" alt="GoHighLevel login" />

### Step 3: Authorize the integration
Once logged in, you're prompted to authorize the connection. Grant the required permissions to enable data synchronization between GoHighLevel and our platform.
