# Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the Google integration:

### Step 1: Go to the Integrations section
Locate and select the **Integrations** section within your application. This area lets you connect with various external services to enhance your email generation capabilities. You see a list of integrations available for connection.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/integra.jpg" alt="Integrations" />

### Step 2: Select Google and connect your account
Click the **Google** integration option to proceed with connecting your Google account. This enables you to use your Google account for email generation.
- Once you connect your Google account, you can use it directly for sending emails.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/sign.jpg" alt="Sign in" />
