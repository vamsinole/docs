## Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the Shopify integration:

### Step 1: Sign in to Shopify
Log in to your **Shopify account**. This ensures you have access to the store you want to integrate with ContactsWing.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/shopify.jpg" alt="Shopify login" />

### Step 2: Go to the Integrations section
Go to the **Integrations** section in the ContactsWing dashboard. Locate the **Shopify card** and click it to start the connection process.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/all.jpg" alt="Integrations page" />

### Step 3: Enter your store name and sign in
Enter your **Shopify store name** when prompted, then click **Sign In**. This initiates the secure connection with your Shopify account.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/shop.jpg" alt="Enter store name" />

### Step 4: Connected!
Once authorized, you see a confirmation message. Your Shopify account is now connected to ContactsWing. You can begin using smart commerce tools in calls, chats, and AI workflows. ðŸŽ‰
