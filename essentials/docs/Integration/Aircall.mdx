# Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the Aircall integration:

### Step 1: Go to the Integrations section
Go to the **Integrations** section in your application. You'll find a list of external services. Select **Aircall** to start the setup process.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/all.jpg" alt="Integrations" />

### Step 2: Enter your Aircall API credentials
To integrate Aircall, provide these details:
- **API ID**: Enter your Aircall API ID from your Aircall dashboard.
- **API Token**: Enter your Aircall API Token for authentication.

After entering these details, click **Connect** to proceed.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/aircall.jpg" alt="Aircall credentials" />

### Step 3: Authorize the integration
Once you enter your Aircall credentials, the system verifies your details. Upon successful authorization, your Aircall account connects, enabling call and voice features in your app.

## ðŸ“ž Using Aircall in Actions

After you integrate your Aircall account, use it in **ContactsWing Actions** to automate outbound calls and trigger workflows.

### Example use cases
- Automatically trigger calls to leads after form submission.
- Use Aircall to follow up on missed interactions.
- Route AI or human-assisted calls via predefined actions.

### How to use Aircall in actions
1. Go to the **Actions** tab in your ContactsWing dashboard.
2. Choose **Aircall Call** as the action type.
3. Configure:
   - The phone number to call.
   - The trigger (e.g., form submission, bot step, etc.).
   - (Optional) Attach an AI assistant to the call.
