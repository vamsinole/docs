# Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to add the SimplyRets integration:

### Step 1: Go to the Integrations section
Locate and select the **Integrations** section within your application. This area lets you connect with various external services to enhance your AI Agents' capabilities. You see a list of integrations available for connection.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/e1.jpg" alt="Integrations" />

### Step 2: Select SimplyRets and connect your account
Click the **SimplyRets** integration option to proceed with connecting your SimplyRets account. This enables you to use your SimplyRets account while creating agents. You need to provide your SimplyRets username and password.
- **API Key**: Enter your SimplyRets username and password.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/e5.jpg" alt="SimplyRets integration" />

### Step 3: Finalize the integration
After you enter the required details (API key and description), click **Connect** to finalize the integration with your SimplyRets account. ✨
