## Why use the Contactswing Extension?

- **Automate responses**: Instantly reply to emails with AI-generated, professional messages.
- **Personalize drafts**: Create tailored email drafts based on context and recipient details.
- **Prioritize messages**: Highlight important emails for quick action.
- **Multi-channel integration**: Respond via email, SMS, or calls from one platform.
- **Save time**: Automate repetitive tasks and manage high email volumes efficiently.

## How to use the extension

1. **Install the extension** from the Chrome Web Store.
2. **Open your email** and let Contactswing suggest or automate replies.
3. **Use the sidebar** to send responses via email, SMS, or call.
4. **Review and prioritize** important messages with AI assistance.
