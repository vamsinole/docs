## Step-by-step guide

### 1. View and manage your phone numbers

See a list of all purchased phone numbers, including:
- **Number**: The phone number in international format (e.g., +1XXXXXXXX).
- **From**: The provider (e.g., Twilio).
- **Status**: Toggle each number between active and inactive.

You can activate or deactivate any number by clicking the switch in the "Status" column.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/p1.jpg" alt="Phone number list" />

---

### 2. Buy a new phone number

1. Click **Buy Phone Number** to open the number selection window.
2. Review the list of available numbers, including:
   - **Phone Number**: The available number in international format.
   - **Region**: Geographic region.
   - **Postal Code**: Where the number is registered.
   - **Price to Purchase**: Cost of the number.
3. Select your desired number and click **Purchase** to add it to your list.

<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/p4.jpg" alt="Buy phone number" />
<img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/helpsessionvideos/Contactswing/images/p2.jpg" alt="Available numbers" />

---

### 3. Verify a phone number

1. Click **Verify Your Number** to open the verification form.
2. Enter the contact's name and phone number.
3. Click **Verify** to confirm the contact's information.
