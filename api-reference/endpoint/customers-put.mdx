---
title: Update Customer
---

**PUT** `/v2/_customers/{customer_id}`

Update a customer by ID.

**Path Parameter:**
- `customer_id` (string, required)

**Request Body:**
- JSON: CustomerRequest (see OpenAPI spec)

**Response:**
- 200: Customer updated successfully
